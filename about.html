We are team 1991!
Our mission is to help students in our school to learn industry level machining and teach about different skills in the workforce.
Our team runs out of University High School of Science and Engineering in Hartford Connecticut.
This season our team has roughly 25 students and 6 mentors.
Our team has previously won 22 awards and has won 3 competitions in 18 years.
We have won awards including “Innovation in Control” and “Gracious Professionalism”.
If you would like to see a full list of our awards, please check out our awards page of our website.
We have most recently won the Hartford Finalist award and won the 2024 CIAC-CAS State Championship.
Our team is small but we definitely manage.
We are always in need of new mentors and/or new companies who are willing to sponsor our team.
